Farm Activities

Experience the charm of our farm through guided tours, workshops, and seasonal events. Learn the art of cheese-making, interact with our animals, and immerse yourself in the farm life.

Farm Tours

Join our guided tours and get a firsthand experience of the farm. Learn about our cheese-making process, explore our facilities, and interact with our happy animals.

Workshops and Classes

Take part in our cheese-making workshops and dairy-related classes. Learn from our experienced artisans and gain hands-on skills in crafting delicious cheeses and dairy products.

Seasonal Events

Stay tuned for our exciting seasonal events held on the farm. From cheese tastings to festivals and farm-to-table gatherings, there's always something special happening at Tiffany's Dairy Farm.
