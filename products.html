Cheese Selection

An overview of the various cheeses offered, including descriptions, flavors, and pairing suggestions.

Our Cheeses:

  • Camembert

    A soft, creamy cheese with a bloomy rind and a rich, buttery flavor.

    Camembert cheese is a classic French cheese known for its smooth and velvety texture. It is made from cow's milk and undergoes a special aging process to develop its distinctive flavors.

    The cheese is characterized by its bloomy white rind, which adds a subtle earthy aroma to the creamy interior. It has a rich and buttery flavor with notes of mushrooms and nuts, making it a favorite among cheese enthusiasts.

    Camembert is versatile and can be enjoyed in various ways. It pairs well with crusty bread, fresh fruits like grapes and pears, and goes perfectly with a glass of dry white wine or cider.

  • Gouda

    A semi-hard cheese with a smooth, caramel-like flavor and a creamy texture.

    Gouda cheese is a Dutch cheese known for its rich and savory taste. It is made from cow's milk and undergoes a unique aging process, which contributes to its distinct flavor profile.

    The cheese has a smooth and firm texture with small, scattered holes. It has a caramel-like sweetness and a nutty undertone, making it a versatile and crowd-pleasing cheese option.

    Gouda can be enjoyed on its own, sliced in sandwiches, melted in grilled cheese, or paired with fruits such as apples or pears. It also pairs well with a variety of beverages, including red wine, beer, and even a cup of tea.

  • Blue Cheese

    A bold and tangy cheese with characteristic blue veins and a creamy, crumbly texture.

    Blue cheese is a distinctive cheese that is known for its strong flavor and unique appearance. It is made from cow's, sheep's, or goat's milk and is characterized by the presence of blue or green veins running through it.

    The cheese has a creamy and crumbly texture, with a rich and tangy taste. The blue veins in the cheese develop during the aging process, thanks to the introduction of specific mold cultures.

    Blue cheese is often enjoyed on its own or used as a flavor enhancer in various dishes. It adds a punch of flavor to salads, dressings, sauces, and even burgers. It pairs well with fruits like pears and grapes, as well as with sweet wines or a glass of port.

Pairing Suggestions:

Enhance your cheese experience with these suggested pairings:

  • Camembert: Pair with fresh baguette and sliced apples.
  • Gouda: Enjoy with a glass of red wine and crackers.
  • Blue Cheese: Perfect when accompanied by dried fruits and honey.

Dairy Products

A section highlighting other dairy products such as butter, yogurt, and cream, along with their unique qualities.

  • Butter

    Our farm-fresh butter is churned from the finest cream, resulting in a smooth and creamy texture. It adds richness and flavor to baked goods, spreads, and sauces.

    • Perfect for baking delicious cookies, cakes, and pastries
    • Spread it on freshly baked bread for a delectable treat
    • Add a dollop of butter to enhance the flavor of your favorite sauces and gravies

  • Yogurt

    Indulge in our creamy and tangy yogurt, made with high-quality milk and live cultures. It's a delicious and nutritious snack on its own or as a topping for granola and fruits.

    • Enjoy it as a healthy breakfast option, paired with your favorite toppings
    • Incorporate it into smoothies for added creaminess and flavor
    • Use it as a versatile ingredient in baking or cooking

  • Cream

    Experience the velvety texture and richness of our farm-fresh cream. It's perfect for adding decadence to desserts, sauces, and hot beverages like coffee or tea.

    • Create luscious whipped cream to top off your favorite desserts
    • Add a splash of cream to enrich your pasta sauces or soups
    • Enhance the flavor of your morning coffee or tea with a touch of cream
